Peacock

Peacocks are polygamous, mating with multiple partners anywhere between two and five throughout their lives.

  • Nickname:Cockie
  • Class:Aves
  • Order:Galliformes
  • Family:Phasianinae
  • Tribe:Pavonini

Peacocks are of bright coloured, with a predominantly blue fan-like crest of spatula-tipped, wire-like feathers.

it has a long beautiful neck and is best known for the long train made up of elongated upper-tail covert feathers which bear colourful eyespots.

These stiff feathers are raised into a fan and quivered in a display durring courtship.
